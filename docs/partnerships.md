# Partnerships

## Built to work with the ecosystem, not around it

Klara does not replace extension services, advisory bodies, or market
networks. It routes users to them at the right moment, with the right
context, so that every interaction is more productive.

### Ecosystem Anchors

**Perennia** — Klara is designed to route users toward Perennia-style
advisory escalation when a grower needs agronomic help on protected
crops, sending them with structured context about what they are growing,
at what scale, and what they have already tried.

**NSCC** — Aligned with NSCC applied-research and greenhouse-training
pathways for user-testing, intake pressure-testing, and prototype-to-pilot
support. NSCC brings structured environments with real domain knowledge.

**Greenhouse Nova Scotia** — Industry association and front door to
the province's structure-supplier and grower network.

### Input & Supply Partners

**Halifax Seed** — Commercial seeds, inputs, structures, irrigation,
growing media, and technical advice. Klara routes qualified,
high-intent buyers to the right product categories.

**Atlantic Canada Greenhouse Supplies** — Structures, coverings,
climate controls, and equipment for commercial and prosumer
operations.

### Market & Channel Partners

**Farmers' Markets of Nova Scotia** — 40+ member markets. Core
infrastructure for excess-supply routing and inventory-gap
rebalancing. Designed to strengthen FMNS food-hub routing channels.

**Nova Scotia Loyal** — Provincial buy-local program. Klara routing
is compatible with Nova Scotia Loyal's buy-local market-access goals,
surfacing provincial producers and channels as the default path.

**Cape Breton Food Hub** — Aggregation, distribution, and online
ordering across the island. A concrete excess-supply rescue lane.

### Institutional Partners

**Nova Scotia Health** — Institutional buyer archetype. The Highland
Health / Summer Street Farm pilot demonstrates protected-growing
supply linked to care pathways and community-health reach.

---

*Klara does not claim partnerships that do not exist. Relationship
status is reported honestly. Where engagement is prospective, it is
labelled as such.*
