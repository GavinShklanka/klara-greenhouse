# Stakeholder Map

Reference: Klara Landscape v1.0.3

## Category 1: Ecosystem Anchors & Technical Partners

- **Perennia** (Protected-crop extension, agronomic escalation)
- **Nova Scotia Community College (NSCC)** (Applied research, greenhouse operations training)
- **Greenhouse Nova Scotia** (Grower network, industry association)
- **Halifax Seed** (Commercial seeds, inputs, structures)
- **NS Department of Agriculture (NSDA)** (Market access, food safety support)
- **Invest Nova Scotia** (Business development, export support)
- **NRC IRAP** (Technical commercialization support)

## Category 2: Market-Shaping & Channel Partners

- **Farmers' Markets of Nova Scotia (FMNS)** (40+ member markets)
- **Nova Scotia Loyal** (Buy-local program)
- **Taste of Nova Scotia** (200+ member culinary association)
- **Cape Breton Food Hub** (60+ local producers, aggregation)
- **Wolfville Farmers' Market / WFM2Go** (Online ordering with pickup hubs)
- **Antigonish Farmers' Market** (Year-round operations)

## Category 3: Institutional & Community-Health

- **Nova Scotia Health (NSH)** (Institutional buyer archetype)
- **Summer Street Farm** (Hydroponic social-enterprise operation)

## Category 4: Capital, Funding & Commercialization Accelerators

- **FarmWorks Investment Co-operative** (Capital pathway for growers)
- **Provincial innovation vouchers** (Applied innovation support)

## Category 5: Commercially Critical Downstream

- **Sobeys, Loblaw, Walmart Canada** (Retail channels via NS Loyal)
- **Sysco, Gordon Food Service** (Food-service distribution)
